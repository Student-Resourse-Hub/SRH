Interview Preparation

Preparing for an interview is key to making a good impression. Here are some strategies to help you succeed:

  • Research the Company: Understand the company's mission, values, and recent news.
  • Practice Common Questions: Prepare answers for common interview questions such as "Tell me about yourself" and "Why do you want to work here?"
  • Dress Appropriately: Wear professional attire that aligns with the company's dress code.
